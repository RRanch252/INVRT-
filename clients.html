Who We Work With

Our advisory approach is specifically designed for leaders who measure success in decades rather than quarters.

Our Clients

We partner with exceptional founders, family businesses, and growth companies to build enterprises designed to create value for generations.

Experienced Founders

Entrepreneurs who have built significant businesses and are now focused on long-term value creation, strategic acquisitions, or thoughtful succession planning. We work with founders who recognize that the next phase of growth requires different approaches than what brought initial success.

Family Offices

Multi-generational investment offices seeking strategic guidance on direct investments, portfolio construction, and governance structures that align with family values. We assist family offices in developing frameworks that balance wealth preservation with generational wealth creation.

Family-Owned Businesses

Established companies navigating the complex intersection of family, ownership, and business dynamics. We provide advisory services for succession planning, next-generation leadership development, and strategic growth initiatives that honor legacy while embracing necessary evolution.

Private Holding Companies

Owner-operated holding structures seeking to optimize their portfolio approach, capital allocation strategies, and management across diverse business units. We help transform collections of businesses into coherent systems that create compounding value over time.
